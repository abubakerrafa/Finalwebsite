Welcome to My World

What started as a simple college spring break trip completely changed my perspective on life. I'm Rafa, a 21-year-old with an insatiable hunger for new experiences and a camera roll that's 90% travel photos (sorry, not sorry).

From the peaceful moments on horseback exploring scenic trails, to the electric energy of Chicago's streets, to finding my zen in the vast silence of desert landscapes – each adventure teaches me something new about myself and the world around us.

This blog is my way of preserving those magical moments that make travel so addictive. From epic fails to life-changing experiences, I share it all here with honesty, humor, and hopefully a little inspiration for your own adventures.

What Drives Me

Authentic Connections

Travel isn't just about places – it's about the people you meet and the friendships you forge along the way. Some of my best travel buddies started as strangers in hostels!

Sustainable Travel

I believe in exploring responsibly. From choosing eco-friendly accommodations to supporting local businesses, every small choice can make a big difference.

Storytelling

Every destination has a story, and I love capturing those moments – both the Instagram-worthy shots and the beautifully imperfect, real-life adventures.

My Travel Journey

2021

The Beginning

First big solo adventure - a road trip across the American Southwest. Fell in love with the open road and realized travel was going to be a huge part of my life.

2022

City Adventures

Explored Chicago for the first time and was completely blown away. The architecture, the food, the people - everything about the Windy City captured my heart.

2023

Desert Dreams

My first desert experience changed everything. The silence, the vastness, the incredible night sky - I discovered a new kind of peace I never knew existed.

2024

Horseback Adventures

Started horseback riding and found my new favorite way to explore. There's something magical about seeing the world from horseback - it's become my preferred adventure style.

2025

New Horizons

Currently planning my most ambitious adventures yet, combining my love for horses, cities, and natural landscapes. Can't wait to share what's coming next!
